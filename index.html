About Me

Hello! My name is Yuri Aoshima. I am a software/embedded systems engineer looking to transition out of the aerospace industry.

I graduated with a degree in electrical engineering with a focus in embedded systems/computer architecture. During my college career I discovered a passion for programming and the amazing ways that lines of code can work together to create applications that people can interact with. After graduation, I pursued a certificate in HTML5/CSS3/Responsive Web Development so I could further my interests and also create my own web applications.

At my current job with The Boeing Company, I work on a wide array of projects spanning from FPGA design to researching IoT and networking applications. However, I am looking to focus more on software development and programming, something I really enjoy, and am searching for a work culture where I am always challenged, always learning and always being pushed beyond my limits.
