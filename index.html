About Me

Hello! My name is Yuri Aoshima. I graduated from the University of Washington with a degree in Electrical Engineering focusing on embedded systems. During my last two years of college I discovered a passion for programming. I find it amazing how lines of code can come together to create interactive applications. After college I obtained a certificate in web development and took classes in C# to advance my skills in programming.

I currently work as an embedded systems engineer at the Boeing Company. I write software drivers for hardware using C in addition to designing hardware. My goal is to go back to school and develop a solid set of skills in higher level programming languages and develop a good understanding of proper software engineering practices. I look forward to life long learning, challenges, and achieving my goals.
